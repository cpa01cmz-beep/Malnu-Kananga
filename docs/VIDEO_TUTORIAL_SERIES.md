## ğŸ¥ Video Production Guidelines

### ğŸ“¹ **Technical Specifications**

#### Video Quality Standards
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1080p (1920x1080)
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Bitrate**: 8-12 Mbps for 1080p
- **Audio Quality**: 48kHz, 256 kbps AAC

#### Recording Setup
```yaml
Equipment:
  Camera: DSLR or high-quality webcam
  Microphone: USB condenser mic or lapel mic
  Lighting: Ring light or softbox setup
  Background: Clean, professional backdrop
  Software: OBS Studio, Camtasia, or similar

Recording Settings:
  Resolution: 1920x1080
  Frame Rate: 30
  Audio: 48kHz 16-bit
  Format: MP4
```

### ğŸ¨ **Production Standards**

#### Visual Branding
- **Color Scheme**: Consistent with school colors
- **Typography**: Clear, readable fonts
- **Logo Placement**: Bottom right corner
- **Lower Thirds**: Name/title information
- **Transitions**: Simple, professional effects

#### Audio Standards
- **Voice Level**: -6dB to -3dB
- **Background Music**: -18dB to -12dB
- **No Background Noise**: Clean recording environment
- **Consistent Volume**: Throughout the video

### ğŸ“ **Content Structure**

#### Standard Video Template
```markdown
1. **Intro (15-30 seconds)**
   - Title card with branding
   - Brief overview
   - Learning objectives

2. **Main Content (80-90% of video)**
   - Step-by-step instructions
   - Screen recordings
   - Clear demonstrations
   - Chapter markers

3. **Summary (30-60 seconds)**
   - Key points recap
   - Next steps
   - Additional resources

4. **Outro (10-15 seconds)**
   - Call to action
   - Support information
   - Brand closing
```

#### Script Guidelines
- **Language**: Bahasa Indonesia with clear pronunciation
- **Pacing**: Moderate, easy to follow
- **Tone**: Friendly, professional, encouraging
- **Length**: Optimal for attention span (3-15 minutes)
- **Chapters**: Logical breaks with clear titles

## â™¿ Accessibility Standards

### ğŸ¯ **WCAG 2.1 Compliance**

#### Visual Accessibility
- **High Contrast**: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio
- **Font Size**: Minimum 14pt for body text
- **Color Usage**: Not the only way to convey information
- **Animations**: No flashing content (3+ flashes per second)

#### Audio Accessibility
- **Quality**: Clear, no background noise
- **Volume**: Consistent throughout
- **Speech Rate**: Natural, easy to understand
- **Background Music**: Subtle, doesn't interfere

#### Content Accessibility
- **Captions**: Accurate, synchronized captions
- **Transcripts**: Full text transcripts available
- **Audio Descriptions**: For important visual content
- **Language**: Clear, simple Bahasa Indonesia

### ğŸ“ **Accessibility Features**

#### Required Elements
1. **Closed Captions**
   - Accurate transcription
   - Synchronization with audio
   - Speaker identification
   - Sound effects description

2. **Transcripts**
   - Complete text version
   - Timestamp references
   - Downloadable format
   - Searchable content

3. **Audio Descriptions**
   - Visual content description
   - Screen reading explanation
   - Action descriptions
   - Navigation guidance

#### Implementation Example
```html

<video controls>
  <source src="tutorial.mp4" type="video/mp4">
  <track kind="captions" src="captions.vtt" srclang="id" label="Bahasa Indonesia">
```
